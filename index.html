FAQ

How does your pricing work?

We offer clients a free website creation service, followed by a monthly subscription for website maintenance. Hosting costs are covered by us, while clients are responsible for domain registration fees. Clients have the flexibility to unsubscribe at any time, but if they decide to part ways and keep the website, they must remain subscribed for at least 12 months.

What if I already have a website?

If you already have a website, we offer two options to enhance your online presence. Firstly, we can upgrade your existing website, providing updates, improvements, and ongoing maintenance to ensure it stays modern and functional. Alternatively, if you prefer a fresh start, we can perform a complete revamp based on the structure and content of your current site. This approach allows us to leverage your existing assets while implementing new design elements and features to meet your evolving needs. Whether it's an upgrade or a revamp, our goal remains the same: to provide you with a polished and effective online platform that reflects your brand and engages your audience.

Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your experience at Bringgrow. However, we understand that sometimes things may not go as planned. Our refund policy is as follows:

Eligibility: Refunds are only available for the current ongoing subscription. This means that if you are not satisfied with your current subscription period, you may request a refund for that period only.

Non-Refundable Items: Subscriptions from previous months or any past subscription periods, as well as any transactions that are older than 1 month, are not eligible for a refund. Once a subscription period has ended, or a transaction is older than 1 month, it is no longer refundable.

Refund Process: To request a refund for your current subscription, please contact us at support@bringgrow.com with your account details and the reason for your request. All refund requests must be made within the current subscription period.

Refund Approval: Refunds are granted at the discretion of Bringgrow. We reserve the right to deny a refund request if we believe the request is not in line with this policy.

How long does it take to build a site?

The timeline for building a website typically varies based on the complexity and specific requirements of the project. However, with our streamlined process and efficient team, we can often complete website development in less than a week. The exact timeframe depends on the level of customization and detail required by the client. For simpler websites or those with minimal customization needs, we can expedite the process to meet tight deadlines. Conversely, more intricate projects with extensive features or content may take slightly longer. Regardless, our commitment to delivering high-quality results remains unwavering, and we strive to accommodate our clients' timelines while ensuring a polished and functional website.

Can I customize my website design and layout?

Absolutely! We prioritize your preferences and vision when it comes to customizing your website design and layout. Our team will meticulously follow your requests, ensuring that the page reflects your unique style and branding. Additionally, we provide a review phase upon delivering the initial website, during which we can collaborate to fine-tune and adjust the layout further, as well as customize any additional details to ensure your complete satisfaction. Your input is invaluable throughout the process, and we're dedicated to creating a website that perfectly aligns with your vision and objectives.

What should you do if you require changes on your page?

If you require changes on your page, simply contact us anytime via email at support@bringgrow.com, and we will promptly take care of it for you.

What does our support offer?

We provide comprehensive support, handling everything from content changes to layout adjustments, uploading images, and videos to ensure your content remains up to date and in line with your preferences.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You can do so from your email receipt, or alternatively, you can contact our support team at support@bringgrow.com, and we can assist you with the process.

If you no longer need our services, how can you keep your website?

If you're subscribed for 12 or more months and decide to part ways with us, you'll gain full ownership of the design and the page itself.
